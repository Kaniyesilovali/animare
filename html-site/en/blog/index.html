Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I take my pet to the veterinarian?

Healthy adult pets should visit the vet at least once a year for a general check-up and booster vaccinations. Puppies, kittens, senior pets, and those with health conditions may need more frequent visits.

What are the most important vaccinations for dogs and cats?

For dogs, the core vaccines include DHPPi (distemper, parvovirus, hepatitis) and rabies. For cats, FVRCP and rabies are essential. Our veterinary team will personalise a schedule for your pet.

What signs should prompt an emergency vet visit?

Seek immediate veterinary care if your pet shows difficulty breathing, uncontrolled bleeding, suspected poisoning, loss of consciousness, or severe vomiting and diarrhoea. When in doubt, always call our clinic.
