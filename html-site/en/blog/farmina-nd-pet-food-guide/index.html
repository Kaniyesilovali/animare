Farmina N&D Pet Food: Is It the Right Choice for Your Cat or Dog?

5 July 2026 · Animare Veterinary

What Is Farmina N&D?

Farmina N&D (Natural & Delicious) is the cat and dog food range of Farmina Pet Foods, a family-owned company based in Italy. The brand is known for formulas that put animal protein first and use low-glycaemic ingredients, and it states that it avoids artificial preservatives. Because it is one of the brands pet owners in North Cyprus ask us about most often, this guide looks at the N&D lines from a veterinarian's perspective.

The N&D Lines: Which One Fits Your Pet?

Farmina N&D consists of five main lines, each representing a different nutritional approach:

  • N&D Prime: The grain-free core line with a high proportion of animal ingredients. A common choice for active, healthy adults.
  • N&D Ancestral Grain: Contains small amounts of low-glycaemic "ancient grains" such as spelt and oats. A balanced alternative for pets that do not need a grain-free diet.
  • N&D Pumpkin: A grain-free formula with added pumpkin to support digestion. Often considered for pets with sensitive stomachs.
  • N&D Quinoa: The functional line — formulas targeting specific needs such as skin & coat, digestion, weight management, and urinary health.
  • N&D Ocean: Fish-based protein sources. Can be an alternative for pets sensitive to chicken protein.

The lines come as dry and wet food, with puppy/kitten, adult, and neutered variants.

Grain-Free or Ancient Grain?

"Grain-free is always better" is a common but mistaken belief. Grain allergies are relatively rare in cats and dogs; most food allergies come from protein sources such as chicken or beef. For a healthy pet, both Prime (grain-free) and Ancestral Grain can be balanced options. What matters is matching the food to your pet's age, weight, activity level, and any existing health conditions.

Choosing Food by Life Stage

  • Puppies and kittens: Growth demands more energy and protein — always choose a dedicated puppy or kitten formula.
  • Adults: Standard adult formulas work for most pets, matched to activity level. For neutered pets, the "neutered" variants help with weight control.
  • Seniors: Joint, kidney, and weight management become priorities; the choice should be guided by a veterinary check-up.

How to Switch to a New Food Safely

A sudden food change can cause vomiting and diarrhoea. Follow a gradual 7–10 day plan instead:

  • Days 1–3: 25% new food + 75% old food
  • Days 4–6: 50% new + 50% old
  • Days 7–9: 75% new + 25% old
  • Day 10: New food only

Watch stool consistency and appetite throughout the transition. If diarrhoea lasts longer than 24–48 hours, call +90 533 844 91 92.

A Vet's Advice on Choosing the Right Food

However good the brand, there is no single food that suits every pet. For pets with skin problems, sensitive digestion, excess weight, or a history of urinary issues, picking the right line makes a real difference — and the wrong one can mask a problem. Frequently Asked Questions

Which country is Farmina N&D from?

Farmina is a brand of Farmina Pet Foods, a family-owned company based in Italy. N&D stands for "Natural & Delicious" and covers the company's cat and dog food ranges.

What is the difference between N&D Prime and Ancestral Grain?

Prime is grain-free and built around a high proportion of animal ingredients. Ancestral Grain includes small amounts of low-glycaemic ancient grains such as spelt and oats. Which one suits your pet depends on age, digestive sensitivity, and overall health.

Should I choose grain-free food for my cat or dog?

Grain-free food is not necessary for every pet. Grain allergies are relatively rare; most food allergies are triggered by protein sources. The choice between grain-free and ancient-grain formulas should be based on your veterinarian's examination findings.

What should I do if my pet gets diarrhoea during a food transition?

Slow the transition down: increase the share of the old food and add the new food in smaller steps. If diarrhoea lasts longer than 24–48 hours, contains blood, or comes with loss of appetite, call our clinic.
