Our Story

Founded in 2026, Animare Veterinary Clinic began its journey with the mission of providing the best care to your companions in the heart of Gönyeli / Lefkoşa, North Cyprus (TRNC). Since then, we have helped thousands of pets recover and thrive.

With our expert team, modern medical equipment, and loving approach, we make every animal feel special.

Animare was born from a vision that unites veterinary medicine with love for animals. Since our founding, we have followed modern medical technologies while never compromising our understanding of treating each animal as unique. Our clinic offers both medical excellence and a warm environment.
