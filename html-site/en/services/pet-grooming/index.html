Pet Grooming

Grooming at Animare takes place within the clinic environment under veterinary observation, meaning any skin conditions, parasites, or health concerns identified during the session are immediately addressed. Our experienced groomer provides bathing, drying, breed-specific trimming and styling, nail clipping, and ear cleaning — combining aesthetic care with a thorough health check for your pet's wellbeing.

  • Breed-specific trimming and styling

  • Ear cleaning
  • Parasite check
