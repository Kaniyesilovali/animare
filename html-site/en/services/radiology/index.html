Radiology

Our clinic is equipped with digital X-ray and ultrasound imaging technology that enables rapid, accurate diagnosis without unnecessary procedures. Digital radiography is invaluable for evaluating bone fractures, joint conditions, and chest pathologies; ultrasonography allows real-time assessment of organ sizes, internal masses, and pregnancy. All images are digitally archived for follow-up comparisons.

Service Scope

  • Digital X-ray (all body regions)
  • Abdominal ultrasonography
  • Pregnancy monitoring
  • Foreign body detection
  • Digital archiving

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment for X-ray or ultrasound?

A general check-up appointment is recommended first. In emergencies, imaging can be arranged immediately.

Is the radiology procedure safe?

Our digital X-ray equipment uses minimal radiation doses. Ultrasonography is entirely radiation-free and safe for all patients.

Can you check pregnancy with ultrasound?

Yes. Ultrasound reliably confirms pregnancy and estimates litter size in dogs and cats.
