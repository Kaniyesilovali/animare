🔭Radiology

Digital X-ray and ultrasound imaging for fast and accurate diagnosis.

Our clinic is equipped with digital X-ray and ultrasound imaging technology that enables rapid, accurate diagnosis without unnecessary procedures. Digital radiography is invaluable for evaluating bone fractures, joint conditions, and chest pathologies; ultrasonography allows real-time assessment of organ sizes, internal masses, and pregnancy. All images are digitally archived for follow-up comparisons.

Service Scope

  • Digital X-ray (all body regions)
  • Abdominal ultrasonography
  • Pregnancy monitoring
  • Foreign body detection
  • Digital archiving
