Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best age for neutering?

Neutering is typically performed from 5–6 months of age for dogs and cats. The exact timing depends on breed and health status.

How should I prepare my pet for surgery?

Food and water must be withheld for at least 8 hours before the operation. Full pre-operative instructions are provided at the time of booking.

How long is the recovery period?

Routine neutering usually requires 7–10 days. More complex procedures may take longer; a follow-up appointment is scheduled for every patient.
