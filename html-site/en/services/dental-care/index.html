Dental Care

Oral health is directly linked to your pet's overall wellbeing. Untreated tartar and gum disease can lead to heart, kidney, and liver problems over time. Our clinic offers ultrasonic tartar cleaning, intra-oral examination, and tooth extraction where necessary — all performed under appropriate sedation protocols to ensure your pet's comfort and safety.

Service Scope

  • Ultrasonic tartar cleaning
  • Gum health assessment
  • Tooth extraction (where required)
  • Home care recommendations

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my pet have dental cleaning?

Professional cleaning at least once a year is recommended. Breeds prone to tartar build-up may benefit from more frequent appointments.

Does dental cleaning require anaesthesia?

Yes, a short-acting anaesthetic is used so the procedure is stress-free and safe. It is performed under full clinical supervision.

What can I do at home?

Our vet will recommend a suitable pet toothbrush and paste. Regular home brushing significantly reduces the rate of tartar accumulation.
