Nutrition Consulting

Proper nutrition is one of the most important factors in your pet's long-term health. Our veterinary team creates personalised feeding plans that account for your pet's life stage, breed, weight, and any existing medical conditions. We stock N&D Farmina premium veterinary foods and can recommend specific therapeutic diets for kidney disease, joint problems, skin conditions, and weight management — ensuring every meal supports your pet's wellbeing.

Service Scope

  • Life-stage specific planning (puppy/kitten, adult, senior)
  • Breed-specific requirement assessment
  • Weight management & diet programme
  • Kidney, joint, and skin support diets
  • N&D Farmina premium food consulting
