Nutrition Consulting

"Suitable for all breeds" on the bag rarely means it's actually right for your pet. Our veterinary team assesses your pet's life stage, breed, weight, and health history to build a feeding plan made for them — whether that's a growing puppy, an overweight adult, or a cat with kidney disease. Recommended N&D Farmina premium foods are available directly at the clinic.

Service Scope

  • Life-stage specific planning (puppy/kitten, adult, senior)
  • Breed-specific requirement assessment
  • Weight management & diet programme
  • Kidney, joint, and skin support diets
  • N&D Farmina premium food consulting

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get nutrition advice for my pet?

Our vet will assess your pet's age, breed, weight, and health status and prepare a personalised nutrition plan during a consultation.

Are N&D Farmina products available at your clinic?

Yes, the premium N&D Farmina range recommended by our veterinarians is stocked at our clinic.

Can you create a weight management plan?

Yes. Our vet designs controlled diet programmes for overweight pets and schedules regular weigh-ins to monitor progress.
